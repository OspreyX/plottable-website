Internet Space Ships!

This complex example mixes several concepts. It uses a scatter plot and projects many different pieces of data onto it, setting position, color, and stroke, and radius. In addition, it uses tooltips.

The data displays different ship classes from EVE Online. The x position corresponds to armor and the y position corresponds to shields. Points are sized according to their type (Battleships are largest and frigates are smallest), and the stroke color indicates whether the ship is Tech I, Tech II, or Faction.

Try zooming in on it or hovering on the data!
